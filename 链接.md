nginx.conf 配置文件位置：/usr/local/etc/nginx/nginx.conf

nginx 安装目录：/usr/local/Cellar/nginx

nginx 网站目录：/usr/local/var/www

启动：sudo nginx
重启：sudo nginx -s reload
退出：sudo nginx -s quit
